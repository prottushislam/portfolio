Who I Am

🌱 Student · Developer · Changemaker

I'm Prottush Islam, a university student at the University of the People and a self-driven developer from Bangladesh. I started coding out of curiosity — and it quickly became my superpower for solving real problems.

My stack spans React, JavaScript, Python, HTML, and CSS. I build web applications that are fast, minimal, and purposeful — from client projects to personal tools that automate workflows with AI.
